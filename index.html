About

I am a Research Director at Crime & Education Lab New York, part of the University of Chicago's Urban Labs.

My research spans the fields of education, crime, labor, and applied econometrics. My work focuses primarily on causal policy evaluation using experimental and quasi-experimental methods, along with a subset of work utilizing structural modeling and estimation.
